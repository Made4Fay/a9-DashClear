## Fragment Collection Protocol

### What Qualifies as a Fragment?

**A fragment is:**
- Something that catches attention without clear reason
- A piece that doesn't fit current categories
- An outlier, edge case, or anomaly
- A connection that feels true but can't be explained yet
- A dream image, synchronicity, or glitch

### Collection Practice:

1. **Notice without judging**
   - Don't ask "is this useful?"
   - Ask "does this shimmer?"

2. **Capture loosely**
   - No rigid structure
   - Tag with time, context, feeling
   - Allow uncertainty

3. **Resist immediate categorization**
   - Don't force it into existing systems
   - Let it stay unresolved
   - Trust that connection will emerge

4. **Review peripherally**
   - Don't stare directly at fragments
   - Let them arrange themselves
   - Notice what gravitates together
